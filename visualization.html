Six Vajrayana Visualization Meditations

1. Guru Yoga

  1. Sit in a comfortable meditation posture. Take a few deep breaths to calm your mind.
  2. Visualize your root guru in the space about a foot above your head, seated on a lotus and moon seat.
  3. See your guru as the embodiment of all Buddhas, radiating wisdom and compassion.
  4. Imagine a stream of nectar and light flowing from your guru into your crown, purifying your body, speech, and mind.
  5. Recite the following mantra 108 times:

    6. OM AH GURU VAJRADHARA HUM

  6. After recitation, visualize your guru dissolving into light and merging with you through your crown.
  7. Rest in the state of union with your guru's wisdom mind.
  8. Dedicate the merit of your practice for the benefit of all beings.

Prayer: "Precious Guru, embodiment of all Buddhas, I take refuge in you. Bless me to realize the nature of mind and benefit all beings."

2. Deity Yoga (Chenrezig/Avalokiteshvara)

  1. Begin with taking refuge and generating bodhicitta.
  2. Visualize yourself dissolving into emptiness.
  3. From emptiness, visualize yourself arising as Chenrezig, with a white body, four arms, seated in vajra posture.
  4. Your first two hands are at your heart in prayer mudra, holding a wish-fulfilling jewel. The lower right hand holds a crystal mala, and the lower left hand holds a white lotus.
  5. Feel that you embody the qualities of compassion and wisdom.
  6. Recite the six-syllable mantra 108 times:

    7. OM MANI PADME HUM

  7. Visualize light radiating from your heart, blessing all beings and purifying their suffering.
  8. Dissolve the visualization and rest in the nature of mind.
  9. Dedicate the merit.

Prayer: "May I embody the compassion of Chenrezig and work tirelessly for the benefit of all beings until samsara is emptied."

3. Vajrasattva Purification

  1. Sit comfortably and generate the intention to purify all negative karma.
  2. Visualize Vajrasattva seated above your head, white in color, holding a vajra and bell.
  3. Recite the 100-syllable mantra once:

    4. OM VAJRASATTVA SAMAYA MANUPALAYA VAJRASATTVA TVENOPATISHTHA DRIDHO ME BHAVA SUTOSHYO ME BHAVA SUPOSHYO ME BHAVA ANURAKTO ME BHAVA SARVA SIDDHIM ME PRAYACCHA SARVA KARMA SU CAME CITTAM SHRIYAM KURU HUM HA HA HA HA HOH BHAGAVAN SARVA TATHAGATA VAJRA MA ME MUNCA VAJRI BHAVA MAHA SAMAYA SATTVA AH HUM PHAT

  4. Then recite the short mantra 108 times:

    5. OM VAJRASATTVA HUM

  5. As you recite, visualize a stream of purifying nectar flowing from Vajrasattva into your crown, purifying all negativities.
  6. After recitation, Vajrasattva dissolves into you, blessing your body, speech, and mind.
  7. Rest in the pure nature of mind.
  8. Dedicate the merit.

Prayer: "Through this purification practice, may all beings be freed from the obscurations of karma and afflictive emotions."

4. Mandala Offering

  1. Sit before your altar or visualize the field of merit before you.
  2. Take a mandala plate or visualize it in your hands.
  3. As you build the mandala, recite:

    4. OM VAJRA BHUMI AH HUM

  4. Visualize the golden base, the four continents, Mount Meru, the sun and moon, and all precious offerings.
  5. Offer the mandala with the following verse:

    6. "This ground, anointed with perfume, strewn with flowers,
    Adorned with Mount Meru, four continents, the sun and the moon.
    I imagine this as a buddha-field and offer it.
    May all beings enjoy this pure land!"

  6. Recite the offering mantra:

    7. OM IDAM GURU RATNA MANDALAKAM NIRYATAYAMI

  7. Visualize the field of merit accepting your offering with joy.
  8. Repeat the offering three times, each time imagining you're offering the entire universe.
  9. Conclude by dedicating the merit.

Prayer: "Through this perfect offering, may all beings accumulate merit and wisdom, and swiftly attain enlightenment."

5. Green Tara Visualization

  1. Begin by taking refuge and generating bodhicitta.
  2. Visualize Green Tara before you, seated on a lotus and moon disc.
  3. Her body is green, youthful, and radiant. Her right foot is extended, ready to step into action to help beings.
  4. Her right hand is in the gesture of supreme generosity, and her left hand holds the stem of a blue utpala flower.
  5. Feel her energy of fearlessness and compassionate action.
  6. Recite her mantra 108 times:

    7. OM TARE TUTTARE TURE SOHA

  7. As you recite, visualize green light emanating from Tara, blessing you and all beings, removing fears and obstacles.
  8. At the end, Tara dissolves into you, blessing your body, speech, and mind.
  9. Rest in the state of fearlessness and compassionate readiness to act.
  10. Dedicate the merit.

Prayer: "May the blessings of Arya Tara eliminate all inner and outer obstacles, and may I swiftly attain enlightenment for the benefit of all beings."

6. Amitabha Pure Land Visualization

  1. Sit comfortably and calm your mind with a few deep breaths.
  2. Visualize before you the Buddha Amitabha, red in color, seated in meditation posture.
  3. He is surrounded by bodhisattvas in a beautiful Pure Land with jeweled trees, lotus ponds, and wish-fulfilling gems.
  4. Feel the peace and purity of this realm.
  5. Recite Amitabha's mantra 108 times:

    6. OM AMI DEWA HRIH

  6. As you recite, visualize red light emanating from Amitabha, purifying your karma and blessing you.
  7. Feel a deep connection to Amitabha and aspire to be reborn in his Pure Land.
  8. At the end, Amitabha and the Pure Land dissolve into light and merge with you.
  9. Rest in the pure awareness of Buddha nature.
  10. Dedicate the merit for all beings to be reborn in the Pure Land.

Prayer: "By the blessings of Buddha Amitabha, may I and all beings be reborn in Sukhavati Pure Land, and from there, swiftly attain enlightenment to liberate all beings."
