Introduction to Meditation

Meditation is a central practice in Buddhism, helping practitioners develop mindfulness, concentration, and insight.

Basic Meditation Instructions

  1. Find a quiet place to sit comfortably.
  2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath.
  3. When your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back to your breath.
  4. Start with 5-10 minutes and gradually increase your practice time.

Types of Buddhist Meditation

  • Mindfulness Meditation (Vipassana)
  • Loving-Kindness Meditation (Metta)
  • Zen Meditation (Zazen)
